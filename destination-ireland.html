Entry Requirements

Indian students need to apply directly to the university or college of New Zealand they wish to take admission in. Following are the requirements which need to be fulfilled by them

Diploma Courses

Completion of 10+2 with at least 55% marks, documents to prove the same and IELTS score - 5.5 band overall.

Bachelor Degree & Graduate Diploma

Completion of 10+2 with at least 60% marks in academics for Bachelor Studies and Graduation with 60% marks for Graduate Diploma Studies and IELTS Score 6.0 bands overall.

Post Graduate & Master Degree Course

Completion of Graduation with 65% marks along with 2-3 years of work experience and IELTS Score 6.5 bands overall.

These are general requirements. Each university has its own, course specific requirements too. Our consultants will guide you through the entire admission process including the formalities for student visa.

Working Rights

DURING COURSE (PART TIME)

Certain changes made to the visa requirements in 2013 made it simpler for international students to work during their stay in New Zealand. These changes have allowed better working rights to English language students who are pursuing their course with high standard providers for working at part-time jobs. This has also given provision for international students with a course of one academic year or more to work full-time during all their scheduled holidays. Working rights are unlimited for international students pursuing their Masters and PhD courses.

AFTER COURSE COMPLETION

New Zealand Student Visa will also allow you to work up to 20 hours a week during your course apart from the full time hours during holidays. Those willing to stay in New Zealand after their course can apply for a Post-study work visa which will permit you to stay for 12 months and work. After this period, you can apply for an employer assisted Post-study work visa which will allow you stay for another 2 years for gaining work experience. You can stay back for longer if you wish by applying for the permanent residency of New Zealand under the Skilled Migrant Category.
